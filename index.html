About


I have over a decade of experience in audio and video production. Working with audio is something I have been constantly passionate about for my entire life. Today I do freelance work for recording, sound design, production, and live performances as a DJ or sound engineer.
For my personal artist projects I focus on synthesis and sound design in music as well as making visual sound reactions or CGI animations for the music. I use a combination of additive, subtractive, FM, wavetable, physical modeling, and resynthesis for my sounds. Each method having unique characteristics.
For video animations I use Cinema 4d for 3d modeling and sound reaction. Mandulbulb 3d for layering 3d fractal formulas and interpolating changes of values in frame sequences timed to the bpm/events of studio tracks. I then post processing everything in Adobe After Effects.
For my Album art I use a combination of Cinema 4d, mandelbulb 3d, neural style transfer, and Photoshop.

About

Blombo – Stuck In The Middle With You

On this project I worked with the Toronto rock band Blombo and two videographers. I set up a multi-track recorder and patched each member of the band in to record each performance simultaneously. After a few takes I then post processed each audio track, mixed them, and mastered for the final result that you hear in the video.

Son Roberts - Keep That Joint Alive

For this live video I had both roles of a live audio engineer and a recording engineer, running a mixer for the PA system that the audience could hear and one multi-track recording console for the video. I then mixed and mastered the tracks and the results are what you hear in the video.

Blombo – Trip To The Edgehub

For the album, I recorded Bombo’s performance for each song to make guide tracks then I isolated each band member and recorded them separately, for the cleanest takes possible, layering each performance one on top of the other. Afterward I mixed and mastered each song for the album release.
