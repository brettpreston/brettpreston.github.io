ENGINEERING AND CONSULTING

AUDIO ENGINEERING CONSIDERATIONS FOR A MODERN MIXNET is a recent analysis on the requirements of real-time voice calling in anonymous communications with ambitious threat models. It focuses on information security and codec engineering.

This work was supported by a grant from the Wau Holland Foundation, as a part of the Katzenpost free and open source project. It is public under a GPLv3 license. The analysis has subsequently helped the project secure an NLnet grant for audio implementation.
